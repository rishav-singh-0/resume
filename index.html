Youniaccess

Youni.com is a campus community for college students. The main core is to help college students find part-time jobs. It also provides activities and discounts for college students.

I came to Youni.com in April 2013. This is a start-up company and very passionate. We spent 5 months from designing to implementation and finally launching. Because there were only 2 people from design to final completion, it took a little longer. I am in charge of design and front-end, and another person is in charge of the entire back-end and database. At the end of September, we went to Beijing together to learn about entrepreneurial experience and find investment partners. Although we could not complete the financing in the end, we still learned a lot.
